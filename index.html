BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a simple tool that helps assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. By inputting your weight and height, the calculator provides a BMI value, which is then categorized into ranges such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity. While useful for general health assessment, BMI doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or fat distribution, so it's best used in conjunction with other health measurements.
